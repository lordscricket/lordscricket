About Us

Lords Cricket is slightly organised, easy to play, 5ish-a-side tennis-ball cricket. It’s fun and it’s pretentious. We keep scores. We keep stats. We keep rolling the arm over.

Lords Cricket is for those of us who love the game but can’t fathom the idea of (a) playing more than about once a month over the summer, (b) training in any form, (c) safety equipment or (d) umpires. We do, however, like the idea of having a cricket “career” – a batting average and bowling strike rate, and particularly, laughing at each other.

Lords Cricket works just fine with players of mixed ages and abilities – and only 10 players are needed for a great game. There’s one simple page of rules.
